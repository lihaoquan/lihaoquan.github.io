Gameplay

  1. 1. There are two decks: the Normal Deck (containing Lumber, Structure, and Action cards) and the Event Deck (containing Event cards).
  2. 2. Distribute 5 cards to each player from the Normal Deck.
  3. 3. At the start of each turn, the player draws 2 cards from the Normal Deck.
  4. 4. A player can perform up to 3 actions per turn.
  5. 5. If a player has more than 7 cards at the end of their turn, they must discard the excess cards.
  6. 6. A card is “In Play” when placed on the board; it is no longer considered “In Hand”.
  7. 7. Building Structures costs Lumber cards; discard the required Lumber cards accordingly.
  8. 8. Structures can be used as Lumber by placing them with Lumber cards.
  9. 9. After every player has taken a turn, draw an Event card from the Event Deck and react to it.
  10. 10. Repeat steps 3–9 until the Event Deck is empty.
  11. 11. The player with the most points at the end (sum of points from Structure & Lumber cards In Play) wins.
